## 2. Target Audience Personas

### Persona A — "O Empresário Atolado" (The Drowning Small Business Owner)
- **Who:** Owner of a micro or small business (1–20 employees), retail, e-commerce, service provider
- **Revenue:** R$100k–R$1.5M/year
- **Pain:** Spending 3–5 hours/day doing things manually — copying data between spreadsheets, issuing NF-e by hand, chasing unpaid invoices, updating stock manually
- **Trigger to buy:** A competitor told them they automated invoicing; they calculated the hours they waste weekly
- **Objection:** "It's too expensive" / "I don't trust technology"
- **What they need to hear:** "300+ empresários já conquistaram o tempo. O retorno médio começa em 30 dias."
- **Where to reach them:** Google Search (intent-based), WhatsApp Business, Facebook, YouTube Shorts
- **Budget sensitivity:** High. Needs ROI framing. Show payback in weeks, not years.

### Persona B — "A Gestora de Operações" (The Ops Manager Scaling Up)
- **Who:** Operations, administrative, or financial manager at a medium business (20–200 employees)
- **Revenue of company:** R$2M–R$30M/year
- **Pain:** Team growing but processes not scaling — reports take 2 days to compile, ERP data is inconsistent, finance team is firefighting
- **Trigger to buy:** Board meeting revealed inefficiency; a failed hiring round made them look at automation
- **Objection:** "We already tried RPA, it broke after 3 months" / "IT needs to approve this"
- **What they need to hear:** "Zero erros em 8 anos. Integramos com Omie, Bling, Tiny sem precisar do seu time de TI."
- **Where to reach them:** LinkedIn, Google Ads (branded + competitor), direct outreach
- **Budget sensitivity:** Medium. Can justify R$3k–R$15k/month if ROI is clear.

### Persona C — "O Diretor de Dados" (The Enterprise Data Leader)
- **Who:** Head of Data, CTO, or VP Engineering at a large company or scale-up (200+ employees)
- **Revenue of company:** R$30M+/year or international company with Brazilian operations
- **Pain:** Data sitting in silos, no reliable data warehouse, analytics team spending 80% of time cleaning data instead of generating insight
- **Trigger to buy:** Data incident, failed BI project, new CDO mandate, or compliance requirement
- **Objection:** "We need a full-time hire, not a consultant" / "Data governance is complex"
- **What they need to hear:** "Snowflake + dbt + AWS. Pipelines that don't break. Data your team can trust."
- **Where to reach them:** LinkedIn thought leadership, conference talks, referrals
- **Budget sensitivity:** Low. Project budgets of R$30k–R$200k are normal.

### Persona D — "O Head de Produto" (The Tech Company Seeking AI Engineering)
- **Who:** Product manager, CTO, or founder at a Brazilian tech company or startup
- **Revenue:** Series A or later, or large product team
- **Pain:** Wants to embed AI (Claude, GPT, RAG) into their product but lacks ML/AI engineering talent
- **Trigger to buy:** Competitor launched an AI feature; investors asking about AI roadmap; team tried Cursor/GPT wrappers and hit limits
- **Objection:** "We need someone in-house" / "How do you keep up with AI changes?"
- **What they need to hear:** "MCP, RAG, Claude API — already building with the latest stack. Engajamento por projeto ou retainer."
- **Where to reach them:** X (Twitter), LinkedIn, GitHub, AI community events
- **Budget sensitivity:** Low. AI engineering retainers of R$15k–R$50k/month are normal for senior talent.

   - `/cases` → [link to case studies page]

4. **Away message (outside hours):** "Olá! Recebi sua mensagem e retorno em até 1 dia útil. Para urgências, descreva seu processo que analiso assim que possível."

5. **Add WhatsApp button:** Add `https://wa.me/55SEUNUMERO?text=Olá%2C%20gostaria%20de%20saber%20mais%20sobre%20automação` to every touchpoint: website, LinkedIn, email signature, Google Business Profile

### 8.2 Lead Magnet Ideas

**Lead Magnet 1 — "Calculadora de ROI de Automação"**
- Build a simple web form (or Google Sheet) where the user inputs:
  - Number of employees doing the manual task
  - Hours per week spent on it
  - Average hourly cost
- Output: "Sua empresa perde R$X/mês neste processo. Em X semanas, a automação se paga."
- Gate: Collect name + email + WhatsApp to receive the full report
- Effort to build: Low (Google Sheets + Tally or Typeform)

**Lead Magnet 2 — "Auditoria Gratuita de Processos" (Free Process Audit)**
- 30-minute call/form where you identify the top 3 automatable processes in their business
- Deliver a 1-page PDF with: current cost of the process, what automation would look like, estimated ROI
- This converts extremely well because it requires minimal effort from the prospect and delivers immediate value
- Gate: WhatsApp or calendar booking form

**Lead Magnet 3 — "Guia: Os 10 Processos que Todo Empresário Deveria Automatizar"**
- PDF, 2–3 pages, simple design (Canva)
- Covers: NF-e, cobranças, planilhas, relatórios, CRM, WhatsApp, estoque, ponto, folha, e-mail marketing
- Gate: Name + email on a simple landing page
- Distribution: LinkedIn, WhatsApp groups, Facebook groups, Google Ads

**Lead Magnet 4 — "Template: Planilha de Controle Financeiro com Alertas Automáticos"**
- A Google Sheet that sends email/WhatsApp alerts when cash flow drops below a threshold
- Useful even without hiring you — but it naturally leads to "imagine if this was fully automated"
- Gate: Name + email

### 8.3 Email Marketing Setup

**Tool recommendation:** Brevo (formerly Sendinblue) — has a free tier up to 300 emails/day, has Brazilian support, and is LGPD-friendly.

**Nurture sequence (5 emails over 14 days) after lead magnet download:**

- Day 0: "Aqui está seu [lead magnet] + uma pergunta"
- Day 2: Case study email — "Como [cliente anônimo] economizou 40h/mês"
- Day 5: Educational — "Os 3 sinais de que você está pronto para automatizar"
- Day 9: Objection handling — "Por que meus clientes ficam anos sem precisar de suporte"
- Day 14: Direct offer — "Que tal uma análise gratuita do seu processo?"

**Newsletter (monthly):** "Conquiste o Tempo" — one automation tip, one case study, one industry news item. Keep it under 300 words.

### 8.4 Contact Form Optimization

The contact form should ask:
1. Nome completo
2. WhatsApp (primary — not email; Brazilians respond faster on WhatsApp)
3. Empresa e setor
4. Qual processo você quer automatizar? (text area)
5. Quantos funcionários trabalham nesse processo? (dropdown: 1, 2–5, 6–20, 20+)

**No field should be labeled "optional"** — every field provides qualification data.

**After submit:** Redirect to a thank-you page (not just a message) that:
- Confirms next steps ("Paulo entrará em contato em até 4 horas úteis")
- Invites to join the WhatsApp community
- Shows a relevant case study while they wait

**Response time SLA:** Under 4 business hours. Speed to lead is critical — a prospect who fills a form is at peak interest for ~24 hours.
